This application adds exo L'Assomption buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from @exo_LAssomption on Twitter.

exo L'Assomption serves Charlemagne, L'Assomption, L'Épiphanie and Repentigny.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM).
https://exo.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-l-assomption-mrclasso-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM), the Autorité Régionale de Transport Métropolitain (ARTM) and exo L'Assomption.

exo L'Assomption was formerly known as RTM L'Assomption sector and MRC L'Assomption (RTCR).
exo was formerly known as Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM) and Agence Métropolitaine de Transport (AMT).

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news (Twitter)
